Getting Started

New to Trackwise? Follow these steps to get up and running.

1

Add a Company

Go to Companies and add the company you are applying to. This links your applications to an employer.

2

Create an Application

Click + Add Application on the Tracker board. Enter the job title, status, salary, date applied, and any notes.

3

Track on the Board

Your application appears on the Tracker board. Drag and drop cards between columns as your application progresses.

4

Log Interviews

Open an application card and add interview rounds. Record the type, date, outcome, and link any contacts such as recruiters or interviewers.
